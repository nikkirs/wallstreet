about   us

hi

WallStreet Media House is a full service event management firm based in Bengaluru, India, that was created by pairing our passion for business and events. We are not party planners, but we bring a fresh, unique approach to the event management industry. Every project is approached to, with meticulous attention to details and obsessive precision. Regardless of the size and scope, we treat your event like a business with clear strategic goals, defined milestones and a comprehensive plan to ensure that your event is delivered on time and on budget.

Our   Services

We provide services like indoor events , outdoor events, sponsor branding and social engine ads.
Indoor events include weekly themes ,exclusive themes and yearly themes.
Outdoor events include market research, concept development , event management and execution.
Sponsor branding includes venue branding and social media branding.
